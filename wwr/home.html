Alberta White Water Rafting

Welcome to the wild waters of Alberta! Get ready for the ultimate adventure as you navigate the stunning rivers of this breathtaking province. Whether you're an experienced paddler or a first-timer, our expert guides will ensure an unforgettable white-water rafting experience through Alberta's rugged beauty. From the adrenaline-pumping rapids to the serene moments of nature’s splendor, every turn of the river offers a new thrill. So, grab your paddle, embrace the rush, and let Alberta's wilderness take you on a ride like no other!

Your adventure starts here!

At our Alberta-based white-water rafting adventure company, we offer a wide range of thrilling experiences for all levels of adventurers. From heart-pounding rapids for seasoned rafters to more relaxed, scenic floats for families or first-timers, there’s something for everyone. Our experienced, certified guides are committed to safety while delivering an exciting, fun-filled ride through some of Alberta’s most beautiful and rugged landscapes. In addition to rafting, we also offer options such as kayaking, multi-day trips for the ultimate outdoor experience, and specialized tours for those looking to explore local wildlife and stunning mountain views. Whatever your adventure style, we promise a memorable and exhilarating experience on the water!

Getting to our rafting base is easy!

From Calgary, simply take the Trans-Canada Highway (Highway 1) west towards the majestic Canadian Rockies. After approximately 90 minutes, exit at the Highway 93 junction and head south, following the signs for Bow Valley Provincial Park. Our base is located just 10 minutes past the park entrance, nestled along the scenic banks of the Bow River. Look for our bright red sign on the left-hand side – you can’t miss it! Ample parking is available, and our friendly staff will be ready to greet you upon arrival. We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before your scheduled trip to check in, get geared up, and receive a safety briefing. See you on the river!
